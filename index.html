3 Cats and a Dog

Ruby Nevers

Ruby is a 13 year old Great Pyrenees. She enjoys pretending that she's a lap dog, at a mere 79 pounds, she has no concept of her weight. She will lounge all day, waiting patiently for her 'walk' on a leash. She loves her outdoor space, five and a half acres for her to roam. The smells of foreign critters initiates her fierce bark. Anything and Everything initiates her bark.

Murphy Nevers

Murphy is a Ragdoll kitty. He's a polydactyl kitty with EXTRA TOES!!! He loves his morning routing of going outside to grab a critter to eat (yep, his idea of crunchies...). He was rescued when he was 4 years old in 2010. He's not your typical Ragdoll, he is terrified of People.

Princess Nevers

So, with lack of photos, and just playing around, you all get to see a picture of Princess. As creepy as this may seem, she is no longer with us. So for the intent of practice, I'm using her. Princess was a pretty tuxedo kitty. She was 11 years old when she passed of Liver failure (poor baby). She had the fluffiest tail of any cat I've ever known. The only girl kitty I've ever owned, she was sort of the mediator of all the boys when they played rough.

Macaroni Nevers

Uh, Macaroni is the same deal as Princess, he is no longer with us. Again, for the intent of finding recent photos of my new-ish living cats, you all get to meet Macaroni. Macaroni was a Super Polydactyl! That's like a normal poly with extra toes, but with a cape. He enjoyed playing in boxes, knocking the christmas tree over, and sleeping on my face. He was one of those asshole ninja kitties that would love you to your face, but try to kill you while you walk down stairs... He died at 7 years old, but he is still loved.
